Who we are

About the Lab

Our Background

Established within the Department of Computing and Information System at Daffodil International University, the AI Research Lab was founded with the mission of making Bangladesh a significant contributor to the global artificial intelligence landscape.

Since its inception, the lab has grown into a vibrant research community that attracts talented students, experienced faculty, and industry collaborators from across the country and beyond.

Our Vision

"To become a globally recognised centre of excellence in AI research — producing transformative knowledge that empowers individuals, industries, and societies."

Research Goals

  • Develop novel ML algorithms for real-world data challenges.
  • Collaborate with international universities and industry partners.
  • Publish high-impact research in top-tier journals and conferences.
  • Mentor the next generation of AI researchers and engineers.
  • Bridge the gap between theoretical AI and practical applications.

Contributions

The lab actively partners with government bodies, technology companies, and non-profit organisations to translate AI research into tools that address societal challenges in healthcare, education, agriculture, and automation.

What we study

Research Areas

Six core domains where our researchers push the boundaries of what AI can do.

Machine Learning

Developing predictive models, supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms, and statistical methods for real-world data challenges.

ML

Deep Learning

Designing advanced neural architectures — CNNs, RNNs, Transformers — for complex pattern recognition and generation tasks.

DL

Computer Vision

Teaching machines to interpret visual data — object detection, image segmentation, facial recognition, and scene understanding.

CV

Natural Language Processing

Enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language — with a focus on low-resource Bangla NLP.

NLP

Robotics

Building intelligent autonomous systems, control mechanisms, and human-robot interaction interfaces for industrial and social applications.

RBT

Data Science

Extracting meaningful insights from large-scale structured and unstructured datasets using statistical analysis and visualisation.
